Born in Mumbai, India.

This love of creating things led me to start learning more about this. I taught myself CSS and advanced javaScript. For the whole summer I'd be coding and creating sample projects on the way.
All of this finally led to a Software Development Internship in 2018 summer. My boss shortlisted me through an online platform and I was hired after a phone conversation. Though there was lot to learn, the fact that I acquired the job showed how much I had learnt. I did struggle through my internship but learnt a lot more!
There are still many things to do and I wish to advance my skills even further by attaining a technical degree.
